About Us

At Angapradesh Home Flavour Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering pure, organic, and handcrafted products that bring the authentic taste of home-cooked meals to your kitchen. Founded in April, 2021, our journey started with the vision of offering high-quality, natural products that are rooted in tradition while promoting a healthy, sustainable lifestyle.

We believe that food should not only nourish the body but also celebrate the rich heritage of Indian culture. Our product range includes Shudh Kachi Ghani Mustard Oil, Desi Sattu, Katarni Chura, Katarni Rice, Besan, and more—each crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients, all sourced from trusted farms.

Our products are:
  • 100% Organic and Pure: Free from preservatives, chemicals, and artificial additives.
  • Nutrient-Rich: Packed with essential proteins, vitamins, and minerals, designed to support a healthy lifestyle.
  • Traditionally Made: We use time-honored methods like cold-pressing and stone grinding to retain the natural goodness and flavor of every product.

Since our inception, we have earned the trust of our customers and have been recognized with multiple awards for our dedication to quality and authenticity. We take pride in ensuring that each product reflects the heart and soul of traditional Indian home flavors, delivered right to your doorstep.

Whether you're looking to add a splash of authentic flavor to your cooking or simply enjoy wholesome, natural ingredients, Desi Home Flavour is your go-to choice for quality, purity, and tradition.

Join us on our journey to bring the best of homemade, organic goodness to your table. Because at Desi Home Flavour, it’s always about good taste, good health, and a taste of home.

Pure Besan - A Versatile Kitchen Staple

Our Pure Besan, also known as gram flour, is made from the finest quality chana (chickpeas) ground to a smooth, fine powder. A key ingredient in Indian cooking, Besan is used in a wide range of savory and sweet dishes. Rich in protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, it serves as an excellent base for batters, snacks, curries, and desserts. Whether you're preparing crispy fritters, traditional sweets, or using it as a thickening agent for soups and gravies, our Besan adds flavor and nutrition to every meal.

Key Features:

100% Pure and Natural: Made from high-quality, organically grown chickpeas, with no additives or preservatives.
Protein-Rich: A great source of plant-based protein, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans.
Versatile Ingredient: Perfect for a wide variety of dishes, from pakoras, dhokla, and chillas to halwa and ladoos.
High in Fiber: Helps with digestion and provides a good amount of dietary fiber, promoting overall gut health.

Our Pure Besan is an essential pantry item for every home, offering countless cooking possibilities while ensuring the health benefits of a natural, wholesome flour. Use it in your everyday meals, or get creative with traditional Indian snacks and desserts for a delightful, protein-packed treat!

Katarni Chura - A Fragrant, Wholesome Delight

Our Katarni Chura, also known as poha or flattened rice, is a unique, aromatic variety sourced directly from the finest Katarni paddy fields. Renowned for its delicate texture and distinct fragrance, this chura is carefully processed to preserve its natural flavor and nutritional value. Known as a versatile staple in Indian households, Katarni Chura is a light, healthy ingredient that can be prepared in various delicious ways, from savory breakfasts to sweet treats.

Key Features:

Authentic Aroma and Flavor: Made from the rare Katarni rice variety, known for its rich, unique fragrance and taste.
100% Natural and Preservative-Free: Our chura is prepared without additives, retaining the pure, natural goodness of premium-quality Katarni rice.
Light and Nutritious: A wholesome, easily digestible source of essential carbohydrates and energy, ideal for a light and satisfying meal.
Versatile and Easy to Prepare: Soak, season, or fry with your choice of spices, nuts, and vegetables to create a range of traditional dishes or innovative recipes.

Enjoy our Katarni Chura in traditional preparations like spiced poha, sweetened dahi chura, or as a base for snacks and fillings. It’s a fragrant, nutritious staple that brings a taste of authentic Indian tradition to every meal, making it perfect for those who seek both flavor and wholesome, natural nutrition.

Desi Sattu - A Nutrient-Packed Powerhouse

Our Desi Sattu is a wholesome, high-energy flour made from premium roasted chana (gram), ground to perfection to retain its rich nutritional profile. Known as a traditional superfood, Sattu is packed with essential proteins, fiber, and minerals, making it a favorite in Indian kitchens for centuries. This finely milled flour offers a subtle, earthy flavor that complements a variety of dishes, from refreshing drinks to hearty breakfasts.

Key Features:

100% Organic and Pure: Crafted from carefully selected, high-quality roasted grams without any additives or preservatives. Nutrient-Rich: A fantastic source of plant-based protein, fiber, iron, magnesium, and other essential nutrients. Energy Boosting: Known for its high satiety, Sattu is perfect for fueling your day and keeping you feeling full longer. Versatile and Easy to Use: Mix into water or milk with spices for a refreshing drink, add to smoothies, or use in savory or sweet dishes for a quick nutritional boost. Enjoy our Desi Sattu as a refreshing summer drink, a delicious porridge, or an energizing ingredient in your favorite recipes. Sattu is more than just a flour—it's a time-honored source of natural, balanced nutrition for a healthy lifestyle.
